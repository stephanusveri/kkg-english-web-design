Our Campus Values

The Values We Give .

We're always ready to help by providing the best learning experience for you. We believe a good place to study can make your life better for lifelong success.

Our vision

To become one of the leading schools of business in Indonesia with a regional and international perspective, with superior quality based on world class university standards, well known by the wider community, and recognized by the business world.

Our mission

Form professionals, entrepreneurs and future leaders who are competent in the field of business with a regional and international perspective, through a quality process of learning, research and community service.

Our faculties

We offer a wide range of faculties ranging from Faculty of Economics and Business to Faculty of Computer and Communication for freshmen and postgraduates. With the option to enroll in a regular class or an executive class.

Our freshmen scholarship

We provide 100%* scholarship for all enrolling freshmen, that can be obtained with just your high school report cards (preferably grade 10 & 11) or your non-academic achievements.
